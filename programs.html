FAQ

Common Questions

Comfortable outdoor clothing, sturdy shoes (no flip-flops), a water bottle, sunscreen, hat, and any personal medication. We provide all activity-specific gear and safety equipment.

We maintain a strict 1:8 guide-to-child ratio for all programs. For younger age groups (5–7), we may have additional support staff to ensure every child gets individual attention.

Yes! All full-day and multi-day programs include nutritious meals and snacks. We accommodate common dietary requirements — just let us know in advance. Vegetarian and Jain options are always available.

Light rain is part of the adventure! We have rain-specific activities planned. In case of severe weather, we reschedule or modify the program. Full refunds are issued for weather-related cancellations.

For the Little Explorers program (Ages 5–7), parents are welcome to accompany their child. For older age groups, we encourage independent participation to maximize the learning experience. We share live photo updates throughout the day.
