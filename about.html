How It Started

Born From a Simple Idea

Latitude was founded with a simple belief: that children learn best when they're outside, hands in the dirt, eyes on the sky, and hearts full of curiosity.

Growing up in Bangalore, we saw how quickly green spaces were disappearing — and with them, opportunities for kids to experience unstructured outdoor play. We started Latitude to bridge that gap: connecting city children with the incredible natural landscapes just beyond their doorstep.

What began as weekend nature walks in Cubbon Park has grown into a comprehensive outdoor education programme serving hundreds of families across Bangalore.

Your Peace of Mind

Safety Standards

Safety isn't a checkbox for us — it's our culture. Here's how we protect your child on every outing.

groups

Small Group Ratios

1:5 guide-to-child ratio for ages 5–7, and 1:8 for ages 8–16. Every child gets personal attention.

medical_services

First Aid & Medical

Comprehensive first-aid kits on every outing. All guides are Wilderness First Aid certified. Medical forms collected for every child.

gps_fixed

GPS Tracking & Updates

Real-time GPS tracking and live photo updates sent to parents throughout the outing via WhatsApp.

security

Insurance & Emergency Plans

Every participant is covered by comprehensive insurance. Emergency protocols with tie-ups to nearby hospitals.

verified_user

Verified Guides

Every guide goes through thorough background verification, police clearance, and reference checks before joining the team.

checklist

Site Risk Assessments

Every outdoor venue is personally scouted and risk-assessed by our team before any child steps foot there.
