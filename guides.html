How We Select Our Guides

Every Latitude guide goes through a rigorous 5-step selection process before leading a single outing.

1

Application & Screening

Thorough background check & reference verification

2

Skills Assessment

Field-based evaluation of outdoor & teaching skills

3

Safety Certification

Wilderness first aid, CPR & child safety training

4

Trial Outings

Supervised sessions with experienced guide mentors

5

Ongoing Training

Monthly workshops & annual re-certification

Our Promise

Your Child's Safety Is Non‑Negotiable

We maintain strict guide-to-child ratios, carry comprehensive first-aid kits, and conduct detailed safety briefings before every outing. Our guides are trained to handle emergencies and are in constant communication with our base team.
