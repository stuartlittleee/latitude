Program Overview

Where Independence
Meets Purpose

Teen Expeditions is our most immersive and challenging program — a multi-day wilderness experience for teens aged 14 to 16 who are ready for real independence and responsibility.

Set in the biodiversity-rich landscapes of Bheemeshwari and Kanakapura, participants undertake genuine wilderness expeditions, contribute to conservation projects, learn wilderness first aid, and tackle complex problem-solving scenarios.

This isn't a holiday camp — it's a transformative experience. Teens return with practical life skills, environmental awareness, and the quiet confidence that comes from proving to yourself what you're capable of.

What We Do

Activities & Experiences

Multiple days of genuine wilderness immersion, conservation, and personal growth.

camping

Multi-Day Camps

Full wilderness immersion over 2–3 days. Teens set up camp, cook meals, manage resources, and experience life unplugged in the forests of Bheemeshwari.

park

Conservation Projects

Hands-on environmental work — trail restoration, wildlife monitoring, native tree planting, and river clean-up. Teens contribute meaningfully to the ecosystems they explore.

health_and_safety

Wilderness First Aid

Basic emergency response training — wound care, splinting, recognising heat exhaustion, and emergency communication. Real skills that could save a life.

psychology

Problem Solving

Complex scenario-based challenges that test critical thinking under pressure — resource management, route planning, and real-time decision-making with consequences.

6:30 PM — Campfire & Night Navigation

Team-cooked dinner, campfire discussion, and an introduction to night navigation and star identification.

wb_sunny

6:00 AM — Dawn Trek

Trail restoration, native sapling planting, or river bank clean-up — real environmental contribution.

psychology

2:00 PM — Problem-Solving Challenge

Day 3 — Reflection & Return
