Program Overview

Where Leadership
Meets the Wild

Outdoor Leaders is our weekend immersion for pre-teens aged 11 to 13 who are ready for a genuine step into the wild. This two-day program takes place in the rugged terrain around Savandurga and Turahalli Forest, where participants tackle advanced treks, learn map-and-compass navigation, and practise real survival skills.

More than just physical challenges, this program is about developing the mindset of a leader — decision-making under pressure, responsible risk assessment, and the ability to support and motivate a team.

Overnight camping under the stars builds independence and self-reliance in a way no classroom ever could. All guided by certified outdoor educators with a 1:8 ratio.

What We Do

Activities & Experiences

Two days of immersive adventure, leadership training, and wilderness skills.

hiking

Advanced Trekking

Multi-terrain hikes with elevation gains through Savandurga's rocky trails. Participants build endurance, learn pacing, and experience the reward of reaching summit viewpoints.

map

Navigation & Orienteering

Real map reading, compass navigation, and orienteering challenges. Pre-teens learn to find their way through unfamiliar terrain using traditional tools — not screens.

shield

Survival Skills

Shelter construction, safe fire craft, water purification basics, and knot tying. Practical skills that build confidence and resourcefulness in any environment.

emoji_events

Leadership Development

Group decision-making exercises, conflict resolution, and team captaincy rotations. Each participant leads a segment of the trek, building responsibility and empathy.
